Frequently Asked Questions

How does the free trial work?

Rhetoriq offers a 3-day free trial when you subscribe. You will not be charged until the trial period ends. You can cancel at any time before the trial ends through your iPhone Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Open your iPhone Settings → tap your name at the top → Subscriptions → Rhetoriq → Cancel Subscription. Cancellation takes effect at the end of your current billing period.

What are the subscription options?

Rhetoriq Pro is available as a Weekly ($2.99/week), Monthly ($6.99/month), or Annual ($29.99/year) subscription. All plans include unlimited rewrites, dialect transforms, and translations.

What is the Dialect feature?

Dialect lets you transform any text into 100+ regional, cultural, and professional speech styles — from Corporate English and Academic Register to Caribbean Patois, Southern American, Gen Z slang, and more.

How many languages does Rhetoriq support for translation?

Rhetoriq supports translation into 75+ languages including Spanish, French, German, Japanese, Arabic, Chinese, Portuguese, and many more.

What does "Humanize AI" tone do?

The Humanize AI tone rewrites AI-generated text to sound natural, conversational, and human — removing robotic phrasing and making it feel like you wrote it yourself.

Is my data private?

Yes. Text you enter is processed by AI to generate rewrites and is not stored or used for training. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
